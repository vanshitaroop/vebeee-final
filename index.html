Bouquet of Services

Public Relations

PR is one of the most organic and sustainable medium that connects the brands/entity to the public using various tools such as media (press), social media, influencer collaborations, cross marketing and cross associations

Event Management

Event management is a part of PR, where we curate unique experiences for the target audience which creates a buzz in the market as well as benefit the brand in terms of brand recall, recognition. We do stand alone events as well as a part of PR retainership

Image management

This is one of the key roles of PR we build an image of the brand as well as the founder and the face of the brand or the organisation. We manage profiles of entrepreneurs, politicians, socialites etc. get them the opportunities to make their image better in every aspect

Social Media and Content Creation

We do end to end social media management which includes making the creatives, posting, boosting to the right target audience

Cross Alignment

We expertise in cross alignment when we bring different brands from different sectors together and create a bundle for the right target audience

Branding

We help start ups with end to end branding, which includes designing the logo, to branding and creating website, stationery etc.

Editorial Management

Brand and the founders go hand in hand where we try and build up the founder's profile too which creates a different media bank for both the brand as well as the founder

Corporate PR Practice

Having worked with a cross section of industry, we have added to our expertise a dedicated wing that will offer corporate PR consultancy to select clientele. Under this service we will especially focus on the image management of the spokes- person and devise innovative platforms that highlight the group in its CSR capacity and its positioning as an industry leader.
